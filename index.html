Miscellaneous Commissions

My usual style, as I spend a lot of time and effort on it, can get expensive. But I draw other things as well!

Other things I draw include (but are not limited to!):

⊹ My sketchy style ⊹

This style is much more rough, and doesn't take advantage of blending out the shading.
I can finish a piece in about half the time as my usual style, so it costs only half as much!
Perfect for character designs.

⊹ PMD inspired text RP icons ⊹

Inpsired by Pokemon Mystery Dungeon's way of stylizing character portraits, I make icons for use in
text rp. Primarily intended for use over discord, you can use a proxy bot such as Tupperbox to set up
each emotion to appear with a different prefix, allowing your character's image to by dynamic and
change depending on the situation. No more happy smiles while they're actually crying!

⊹ Emotes ⊹

I can make emotes for Discord, Twitch, or other platforms! All emotes can be used as a base to create new
ones, so new/unique ones will cost more, while expressions I've drawn before will come at a dicount due to
less work needing to be done. Ask me to see my full list, or to discuss buying an emote I can't use as a base
for future commissions.

⊹ Other Styles ⊹

I can draw in a variety of different art styles! Two with price examples are shown above, but I have other
examples, and can also draw styles you send me, icluding most animes and cartoons. Some, however, like Arcane,
may be too advanced, so please contact me with a yes or no, and I can let you know if it's possible.
